# 🎬 How to Use Loopy

## Quick Start Guide

### 1. **Sign In** 
- Go to the Login page
- Click **"Sign in with Google"**
- Currently in demo mode (simulates authentication)
- You'll be signed in as "Demo User"

### 2. **Browse Content**
- Explore documentaries across multiple categories
- Scroll through video rows
- Hover over videos to see previews and actions

### 3. **Watch Videos**
- Click any video card to watch
- The AI starts learning your preferences immediately
- You'll see toast notifications as it tracks your viewing
- After 3 seconds: "Learning your preferences"
- Check the "AI Insights" button for content summaries

### 4. **Experience Personalization**

#### First Time (No History):
- All categories shown in default order
- No personalized recommendations yet

#### After Watching 1-2 Videos:
- Return to Browse page
- See **"Personalized for You"** banner
- **"Recommended for You"** row appears
- Categories you watched appear first

#### After Watching More:
- Categories reorder based on favorites
- Related videos ranked higher
- Smarter recommendations

### 5. **View Watch History**
- Click your profile avatar (top right)
- Select **"Watch History"**
- See all watched videos with progress bars
- Clear history to reset recommendations

### 6. **Use AI Search**
- Click **"AI Search"** button in header
- Type questions like:
  - "Climate change effects on wildlife"
  - "Black hole formation"
  - "Ancient Egyptian architecture"
- Get AI-generated summaries
- Jump to exact timestamps in videos
- Click trending searches for quick results

To start fresh:
1. Click profile avatar
2. Select "Watch History"
3. Click "Clear History"
4. Refresh the page
5. You're back to a clean slate!
